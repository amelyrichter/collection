  • Hadal Zone  (6,000-11,000 meters) 
    More people have been to the Moon than the Hadal Zone. Life here is sparse - the extreme conditions make survival difficult. The most notable is the 2012 solo dive by filmmaker James Cameron, who descended to nearly 36,000 feet (about 10,900 meters) in the Mariana Trench, which is part of the Hadal Zone.
